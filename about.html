About ImageStack

Our Mission

At ImageStack, we are committed to simplifying the process of architectural demonstration for Solutions Architects. We aim to provide an intuitive, hassle-free interface where architects can quickly spin up demos for their clients, thereby maximizing their productivity and effectiveness.

Our Product

Our user-friendly web application allows you to upload and share images securely using the power of AWS S3. We provide a selection of pre-configured architectures that architects can choose from to match their client's requirements. By leveraging the powerful and scalable infrastructure of AWS, we ensure that your demos run smoothly and efficiently.

Why ImageStack?

ImageStack allows you to focus on what you do best - building and demonstrating solutions. Leave the complexities of managing infrastructure and file storage to us. With ImageStack, creating impressive demos for your clients is only a few clicks away.
